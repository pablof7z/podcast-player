# Wiki Configuration

## Scope

This topic wiki captures the experience principles and surfaces that make Podcastr feel like a polished podcast app with a powerful knowledge agent, not a generic chat interface.

## Conventions

- Prioritize actual podcast workflows over marketing screens.
- Keep voice, playback, search, and citations visible in product surfaces.
- Accessibility and audio-first use are core requirements, not cleanup tasks.
