## Findings

Podcasting 2.0 is a broad metadata layer, not just transcripts. Its tags support chapters, soundbites, person credits, podroll recommendations, ActivityPub-style social comment roots, live items, locations, funding, value payments, and alternate enclosures.

NotebookLM's Audio Overview pattern is relevant because it treats source-grounded synthesized audio as a first-class artifact. Its interactive mode lets the listener ask questions during the overview and then resume the original audio.

Readwise and Obsidian integrations show that users value append-only knowledge exports, highlights, tags, notes, and custom Markdown templates. Podcast highlights should behave like serious reading highlights.

OP3 suggests a privacy-friendly analytics path: public, open podcast download metrics without building an invasive tracking system.

Apple's App Intents, widgets, Live Activities, controls, Lock Screen controls, Control Center controls, and Action Button integrations can make Podcastr feel ambient and system-native.
